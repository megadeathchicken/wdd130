Willdwater Journey, Co.

Born from a love of the river and a passion for shared experiences, we are more than just a rafting company—we're a gateway to adventure, connection, and the wild beauty of the Nantahala river. Our mission is simple: to bring families, friends, and thrill-seekers together on the water, where every rapid tells a story and every journey leaves a lasting memory. Rooted in tradition and driven by a respect for nature, we ride the currents with purpose—ensuring the rivers we cherish today will be here for generations to come.

History

In 1972, nestled in the heart of the rugged Appalachian Mountains, Willdwater Journey, Co. was born. Founded by outdoors enthusiast and former river guide, Finnigan "Finn" Willd, the company began with a simple mission: to share the thrill of white-water rafting with people from all walks of life, while prioritizing safety, fun, and connection to nature. His passion for the region's waterways lives on through the company, ensuring future generations can experience the thrill and beauty of the Nantahala River.

Finn, a Bryson City native, grew up along the banks of the Nantahala, spending his childhood exploring the winding currents and learning the secrets of the river. After years of guiding rafting tours for larger companies, he realized there was something missing: a sense of personal connection. Finn dreamed of a company where families could come together, share laughter, and bond over a shared adventure, all while cultivating a deep respect for the environment.

Thus, Willdwater Journey was founded—rooted in values of family, community, and wholesome outdoor fun. Over the years, Willdwater Journey has become known not only for its thrilling white-water expeditions but also for its welcoming, family-oriented approach. Each trip is designed to cater to adventurers of all ages and experience levels, ensuring that everyone—from toddlers to grandparents—can enjoy the beauty and power of the river together.

With an emphasis on environmental responsibility, Willdwater Journey has led the way in sustainable tourism practices, ensuring the rivers remain pristine for generations to come. Today, the company continues to thrive under the guidance of Finn's children, who uphold their father's original vision. For them, the river is not just a business—it's a way of life.
